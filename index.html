The Rules

Welcome to our football game, where you can create any team you want!

A real transfermarkt awaits you here: you can buy and sell players. Of course, you need money to do this. In this case, the players are worth a certain number of stars. To get stars, you need to study well, because grades of "5" are exchanged for stars. The course is shown below.

Exchange Rate

You can exchange "5" for stars (a score of "4" does not count :)) Here is the exchange rate of the fives to the stars:

  • 10 fives = 1 star ⭐
  • 50 fives = 5 stars ⭐
  • 100 fives = 10 stars ⭐
  • 500 fives = 50 stars ⭐
  • 1000 fives = 100 stars ⭐ and so on.

Manage the stars wisely! By purchasing a player, you will be able to sell him only at a reduced price (approximately -20%). Think carefully before making a transfer. You can buy a lot of players who are cheap, or save for a long time for a couple of expensive players. It all depends on your scheme and tactics. Study well, get fives and then stars, buy players and create your own dream team!
